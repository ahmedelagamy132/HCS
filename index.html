How HCS works

Three layers of intelligence operating in concert to protect what matters most to you.

01

Computer Vision Layer

Stable-mounted cameras continuously monitor posture, gait, movement patterns, and behavioral cues. Our vision models are trained specifically on equine anatomy and can detect distress, lameness, or restlessness invisible to the human eye.

02

Biosensor Data Stream

The HCS Collar — worn lightly around the neck — continuously streams heart rate, core body temperature, respiratory rate, and 6-axis motion. Medical-grade sensors calibrated for equine physiology. No shaving, no adhesive patches.

03

AI Analysis & Alert

Both data streams merge in our neural network, which runs a continuous health assessment every 3 seconds. Any anomaly — a fever developing, an irregular heart pattern, abnormal inactivity — triggers an instant alert to your phone and your vet.

The Device

Precision engineered
for equine life

Designed around the horse — not adapted from human wearables. The HCS Collar is lightweight, fully waterproof, and built to endure the stable environment. Your horse won't know it's there.

01

Optical Heart Rate Sensor

Medical-grade PPG sensor tuned for equine coat thickness and pigmentation variation. Delivers ±2 BPM accuracy across all coat colors and conditions.

02

Infrared Temperature Array

Continuous sub-surface body temperature measurement. Catches fever onset hours before external symptoms appear — when early treatment matters most.

03

6-Axis Motion Intelligence

Accelerometer and gyroscope capture movement quality to distinguish rest, walk, trot, and canter — and flag lameness patterns, colic restlessness, or irregular lying behavior.

04

72-Hour Battery + Dock

Magnetic charging dock mounts on any stable post. Swapping collars takes under 10 seconds. Always covered.
